Commands.Players.RP.Description:	Toggle receiving realm points
Commands.Players.RP.Usage:			Usage: '/rp <on|off>'
Commands.Players.RP.On:				Your rp flag is ON. You will gain realm points. Use '/rp off' to stop gaining realm points.
Commands.Players.RP.Off:			Your rp flag is OFF. You will no longer gain realm points. Use '/rp on' to start gaining realm points again.
