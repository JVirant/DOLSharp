# /Autoloot
Commands.Players.Autoloot.Description: Automaticly pick up any loot that drops in your area
Commands.Players.Autoloot.Usage: '/autoloot <on|off>'
Commands.Players.Autoloot.Off:	Your autoloot is off. You will no longer attempt to automatically loot anything that drops in your area.
Commands.Players.Autoloot.On:	Your autoloot is on. You will now attempt to automatically loot anything that drops in your area.
