# /checkappeal command
Commands.Players.CheckAppeal.Description:	Checks the status of your appeal or cancels it.
Commands.Players.CheckAppeal.Usage.Appeal:	Use /appeal to file an appeal.
Commands.Players.CheckAppeal.Usage.View:	/checkappeal view - View your appeal status.
Commands.Players.CheckAppeal.Usage.Cancel:	/checkappeal cancel - Cancel your appeal and remove it from the queue.
