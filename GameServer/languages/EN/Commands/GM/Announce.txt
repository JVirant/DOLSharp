# /announce Command
Commands.GM.Announce.Description:			Send message to all players online
Commands.GM.Announce.Usage:					/announce <log|window|send|center|confirm> <message>
Commands.GM.Announce.LogAnnounce:			[Announce]: {0}
Commands.GM.Announce.WindowAnnounce:		Announce from {0}
Commands.GM.Announce.SendAnnounce:			[Announce]: {0}
Commands.GM.Announce.ConfirmAnnounce:		Announce from {0}: {1}
